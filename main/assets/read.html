DICE-ROLL: HOW TO PLAY ?

-> Select the number of rounds you want to play.

-> Rolling the dice for selected number of attempts, you get a number from 1-6. If your dice number is greater than PC's , you get 1 point!

-> After final round, if your points are higher than PC, you win. If it's equal, it's a draw. Otherwise, you lose. :(
